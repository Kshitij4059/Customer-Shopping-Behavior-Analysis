📊 # Customer-Insights-Analysis

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior using Python, PostgreSQL, and Power BI to generate actionable business insights.

🔹 Dataset Overview
Rows: 3,900
Columns: 18
Includes customer demographics, purchase details, and shopping behavior
Missing values in Review Rating handled using median imputation
🔹 Tools & Technologies
Python: Data cleaning, preprocessing, feature engineering
PostgreSQL: Business queries and analysis
Power BI: Interactive dashboard visualization
🔹 Key Analysis
Revenue comparison by gender
Subscriber vs non-subscriber spending
Top-rated and most purchased products
Discount usage impact on sales
Customer segmentation (New, Returning, Loyal)
Revenue contribution by age group
🔹 Key Insights
Discounts influence purchase behavior but need optimization
Loyal customers contribute significantly to revenue
Certain products and categories consistently perform better
Subscription status impacts overall spending
🔹 Business Recommendations
Improve subscription benefits to increase retention
Introduce loyalty programs for repeat customers
Optimize discount strategies for better margins
Focus marketing on high-performing products and customer segments
